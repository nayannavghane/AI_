AI for Teachers

AI can streamline administrative tasks and enhance the teaching experience, allowing you to focus on your students.

  • Lesson Planning: AI can help you generate new lesson ideas, create custom quizzes, and find supplementary materials.
  • Paper & Homework Grading: AI tools can automate the checking of multiple-choice tests and provide preliminary feedback on essays.
  • Personalized Instruction: Use AI to identify which students are struggling with specific concepts and create targeted exercises.
